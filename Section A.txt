#### How It Works

A DDoS attack leverages multiple compromised computer systems as sources of traffic, often using a botnetâ€”a network of hijacked internet-connected devices. These machines send simultaneous requests to the target, causing it to slow down or crash entirely.

#### Types of DDoS Attacks

* **Volumetric Attacks**: Overwhelm bandwidth with data floods.
* **Protocol Attacks**: Exploit weaknesses in network protocols.
* **Application Layer Attacks**: Target specific applications (e.g., HTTP, DNS).

#### Notable Incidents

* In **2016**, the Mirai botnet attacked Dyn DNS service, disrupting major websites like Twitter, Netflix, and Reddit.

#### Impact
